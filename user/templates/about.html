ABOUT
Vision
The purpose of Timeline IL PS is to keep track of the chronological order of daily events happening in Israel and Palestine so it is clear what is happening on the ground, how cycles of violence begin and what the reality of life is for those who are affected by the conflict.
News & Sources
The timeline will highlight all of the following types of events:
Relevant domestic & international news
Acts of violence & aggression
Commentary by key government/notable figures that is directed towards or affects the lives of those on the other side.
Videos & images that show first hand accounts of the conflict
The timeline will utilize the following sourcing methodology:
Primary – ranked as “high” to “very high” for factual reporting on mediabiasfactcheck.com
Examples: ABC News, Associated Press, BBC, Der Spiegel, Haaretz, Le Monde, Reuters, Times of Israel
Secondary – ranked as “mixed” to “mostly factual” for factual reporting on mediabiasfactcheck.com.
Examples: Al Jazeera, Bloomberg, CNN, Jerusalem Post, MSNBC, The Intercept

Verified feeds from social media and above news sources
